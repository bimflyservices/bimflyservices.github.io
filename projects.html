BIMfly supports projects where coordinated MEP models and clear information management are critical to delivery. Below is a snapshot of the sectors we focus on and the type of BIM support typically provided.

Sector ¬∑ Aviation
Aviation Campuses & Terminals
Information Management ¬∑ MEP 3D Modelling

From terminal lounges to support buildings, aviation assets rely on resilient MEP systems and reliable project data. BIMfly focuses on robust models and clear information requirements that work across consultants, contractors and client teams.

  • ISO 19650-aligned information management for airside and landside zones
  • MEP 3D models for terminal buildings, lounges and technical areas
  • Federated coordination with architecture, structure and specialist systems
  • Health checks and dashboards to keep models issue-free over long programmes

Sector ¬∑ Mixed-use
Mixed-use Districts & Energy Centres
MEP 3D Modelling ¬∑ BIM Content Library

Mixed-use schemes combine retail, office, residential and public space within one coordinated model. BIMfly helps keep these environments aligned by pairing clean, reusable BIM content with consistent MEP modelling standards.

  • MEP models for central plant rooms, podiums and tower zones
  • Standardised BIM families and templates for repeated typologies
  • Support for phased delivery, shell & core and fit-out stages
  • Clear drawing and model outputs for on-site coordination meetings

Sector ¬∑ Residential
High-end Residential & Villas
MEP 3D Modelling

Luxury villas and high-end apartments demand services that are discreet but accessible. BIMfly develops coordination-ready MEP models that respect tight ceiling zones, bespoke joinery and complex interiors.

  • Detailed mechanical, electrical and public health models
  • Close coordination with interior design and architectural teams
  • Support for design changes and on-site clashes during construction
  • As-built updates and models ready for facilities management
